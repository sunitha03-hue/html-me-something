1. Masala Vadai

Ingredients

Chanadal - 200g or 1 cup, Cinnamon Stick - 1 inch, Chopped Ginger - 1/2 tsp, Garlic - as required, Fennel/Saunf - 1 tsp, Coriander Leaves - 1 sprig, Green chilies - 1 or 2, Mint Leaves - 3 sprigs, Chopped onion - 1 small, Curry Leaves - 1 sprig, Salt - as required, Oil - for deep fry

Steps

Wash chana dal. Soak it in 1/2 - cup of water for 1 hour. Chop the green leaves. Chop the green chilies. Chop the Onions. Drain the water to a bowl, use this water for grinding, if required. Keep aside for 10 - 20 minutes. Add ginger and few pieces of garlic into a mixer jar. Add cinnamon, fennel seeds to the garlic paste to make it flavor good. Add chana dal to a mixer jar. Add 1/2 Tsp of onion pieces and 1/2 of green chilies. Add ginger-garlic paste and salt. Grind the items into a fin coarse paste. Add chopped onions, green leaves, and chilies to the mixture. Add 1-2 Tsp of oil and add it to the mixture. Mix. The Masala vada mixture is ready. Flatten the vada ball to desired size and thickness. Heat oil for deep frying in a kadhai. Keep the heat to medium. Carefully and gently drop the flattened mixture, one at a time into the hot oil. Flip when the color changes to light golden brown. Turn the heat to low, when both the sides turn golden brown. Enjoy your Crispy Masala Vadai.

2. Tamarind Rice

Ingredients

Mustard seeds - 1 Tsp, Urad Dhal - 1 Tsp, Channa Dhal - 1 Tsp, Curry Leaves - 1 String, Red chilli - 1, Peanuts - 2-3 Tbsp, Tamarind - 1 Amla size soaked in 1 cup of water, Turmeric - 1/4Tsp, Salt Required, Oil - 2-3 Tbsp (Gingely Oil Preferred).

Do nor burn it. Now add coriander seeds to the same pan. Don't add any oil. Toss it till there is a colour change. Add channa dha;. Roast it till golden brown. Add less then 1/4Tsp of fenugreek seeds. Add 1Tsp of pepper. Once cooled make a fine powder. Add gingely oil to a pan. When the oil is hot add Mustard seeds, urad dhal, Channa dhal, Rosated peanuts and curry leaves. I missed the seasoning part alone. I am really sorry. Now add tamarind water to this. 3. Curd Rice

Ingredients

1 cup of rice, 2-2½ cups of water, ½ cup of milk, Plain or unflavored yogurt, 1 tablespoon of oil, ½ teaspoon of mustard seeds, ¼ teaspoon of grated ginger, Few sprigs of curry leaves, 1 green chili (split lengthwise), Pinch of salt, Grated carrot/pomegranate seeds (optional).

Steps

Prepare the rice with excess water to obtain slightly overcooked rice, meaning that the rice is soft and slightly clumped together. When the rice is still hot, mash lightly with a spoon. Now comes the tempering process that takes your normal curd rice to the next level! Heat the oil in a pan and add the mustard seeds when the oil is sufficiently hot. Once the mustard seeds begin spluttering, add the chili, grated ginger and curry leaves. Slowly add the milk to your rice curd mixture until the desired consistency is reached. Add the tempered mustard seeds and chili, mixing well. Sprinkle some salt to taste, and you’re finished! You may also add some pomegranate seeds or grated carrots if you wish!
